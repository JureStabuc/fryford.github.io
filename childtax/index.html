Which tax-free childcare scheme should I go for?
I've recently been trying to work out what tax-reducing childcare scheme to go for. There are two available to me. The first was to use the new government tax-free childcare scheme which effectively gives you back 20% of your childcare costs. The second is the more established scheme run via employers where some of your salary goes directly towards purchasing childcare vouchers. You don't have to pay tax or national insurance on that amount, so you save.
